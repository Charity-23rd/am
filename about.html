About Amazon

Amazon is an Europian jewellery manufacturer and retailer founded in 1990 by Juliet Smith.The company started as a family-run jewellery shop in Monaco.Amazon provides a universe of hand-finished and modern jewellery primarily made from genuine materials, including solid sterling silver and 18k gold. We use a variety of gemstones, stones and cultured pearls to add sparkle and color to our genuine silver and gold jewellery.

Amazon is known for its customizable charm bracelets, designer rings, earrings, necklaces and watches made from high-quality materials at affordable prices. Amazon jewellery is sold in more than 50 countries on three continents (Europe,Asia & Africa) inclusive, through around 4,500 points of sale, including more than 1000 concept stores.
